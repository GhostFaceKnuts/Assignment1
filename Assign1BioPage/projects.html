Though I did not design or create this webpage I have recently taken on the role of administration for www.http://sweetbearbaits.com. Please click the link and check it out for yourself.

I am excited to start working on an online web-based role-playing game. It will be a GitHub project, in collaboration with other web developers, programmers and graphic design students.

What can you expect? Well, the idea is to have a graphical representation of inventory and character (think Blizzard Studio's Diablo 3) with the gameplay style of Kingdom of Loathing.

While you can adventure alone, we want this to be a social game where you can connect with friends and go kill kobolds...or whatever...together. Having the nostalgia of early text-based RPGs and the modern visualizations that gamers have come to expect, I think this is going to be awesome!
